## FAQ

#### What is Project Elric?

Project Elric is an entity that aims to bring people who are eager to improve this technological world we live in.

We want to build stuff that others (and ourselves, ofcourse) would enjoy using without having to worry about their data being collected or sold. We are open not only to the tech gurus, but also the newcomers who are interested in technology and learning new things. The more the merrier.

Keep in mind that we are by no means "professionals". We just try to do what we enjoy.

Let's build while learning, and learn by building.

#### So, everything is going to be free, right?

Well, yes but no.

We support open source and free (as in freedom) softwares. Therefore, we will try to keep everything accessible, ad-free, secure and open sourced.

However, some of our projects (such as video games, which require so much workforce and resource) may be proprietary and offered with a price (one-time payment with lifelong access including free updates, definitely no subscription or additional fees).

Regardless of the project being proprietary or not, we will always respect the privacy and comfort of our users. This comes first.
